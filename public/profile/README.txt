===========================================
HOW TO ADD YOUR PROFILE PHOTO
===========================================

1. Copy your photo to this folder:
   /public/profile/dulanjaya.jpg
   
   Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp

2. Recommended: Square image, at least 400x400px
   for best results in the circular hero display.

3. If no image is found, the site shows a 
   beautiful "DT" gradient avatar automatically.

===========================================
HOW TO ADD YOUR IMS TRANSCRIPT PDF
===========================================

1. Copy your transcript PDF to:
   /public/transcripts/ims-transcript.pdf

2. The "View Transcript" button on the 
   Education page will open it in a modal viewer
   with Download + Open in new tab options.
